About / Science Explanations

Welcome to Asteroid Harvester, a VandyHacks3 project!

Overview
Asteroid Harvester is an always-changing, real-time educational web app that lets users learn about asteroids, astronomy, and more! Travel through space, hunt down real asteroids, and harvest their rare minerals to level up and find new asteroids.
APIs + Real-time
AH utilizes APIs from Initial State, NASA, and (other asteroid database here).
These provide the data seen in-game; the asteroids whizzing by are real, documented asteroids that are concurrently observable in space and game.

Measurements/Units
Lunar Distance (LD), is the average from the center of the Earth to the center of the Moon, which is approximately 250,000 miles.

Astronomical Units (au), is the typical unit in astronomy used for measuring distances within our Solar System. It is approximately the distance from the Earth to the Sun, or 93 million miles.
Which is ~1.6 billion American football fields... Or, 3,734 circumfrences of the Earth!
