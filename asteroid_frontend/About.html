About / Science Explanations

Welcome to Asteroid Harvester, a VandyHacks3 project!

Overview
Asteroid Harvester is an always-changing, real-time educational web app that lets users learn about asteroids, astronomy, and more! Travel through space, hunt down real asteroids, and harvest their rare minerals to level up and find new asteroids.
APIs + Real-time
AH utilizes APIs from Initial State, NASA, and Asterank.
These provide the data seen in-game; the asteroids whizzing by are real, documented asteroids that are concurrently observable in space and game.

Units
Lunar Distance (LD), is the average from the center of the Earth to the center of the Moon: ~250,000 miles.

Astronomical Units (au) is used for measuring distances within our Solar System. It is the distance between the Earth and the Sun, or 93 million miles.
Which is ~1.6 billion football fields, or, 3,734 circumfrences of the Earth!
